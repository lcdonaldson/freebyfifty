    What happened to accountability?

    $#!* happens

    So one of the reasons I stated for this blog was accountability to post content regularly and on time. Then right after my last post I got sick. Not like hey, taking the day off sick. Like, let's get our affairs in order kinda sick. It was a combination of several things, but essentially like getting the flu + sinus infection + ghandi's 7 day diet challenge all at once. It was the kinda sick you wouldn't really wish on your enemies. And it had lasting effects. It had me down for several weeks.

    Then just as things were looking better. I had the distinct pleasure of gowing through floods....in Arizona and New Mexico. Yeah, you read that right, floods, 100 year type floods. Taking bridges, happiness and the desire to blog with it.
