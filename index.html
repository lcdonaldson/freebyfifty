    Freedom of all the things that matter

    At present this site SUPER simple. But I decided it couldn't wait any longer to launch. New features will show up often and by years end this will be the full featured blog it was intended to be.

    Money alone (although the important keystone) just wont give you total freedom. Being free isn't a one size fits all set of steps. A recent article said 20% of people making 250 thousand dollars a year are living paycheck to paycheck.

    The important thing is getting what YOU need from life to be truly free. Financial freedom gives you the peace of mind to achieve freedom in these three major areas of your life.

    • Body

      Whether its losing a few pounds, achieving healty blood test levels, or getting triathalon level jacked. reaching the goals that you have will enable you to enjoy that freedom for the longest time you can.

    • Mind

      One of the greatest miracles of mankind is the ability for people to reach things physically beyond their grasp. Riches can be almost stumbled upon...but wealth is not accidental. Wealth is first and always created in the mind.

    • Soul

      Although money has the power to corrupt, by contrast it also has the incredible power to create things that others can't create for themselves. Is has saving power, relief, healing and peace.
